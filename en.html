About us

New Horizon Duo

Duo of Accordion Virtuosos. Both present a captivating musical journey of sounds, scents, and styles - a rich repertoire of pieces symbolizing milestones from around the world. Starting with Tango (in the style of Piazzolla), Brazilian Samba, through French music (Edith Piaf), and famous classical compositions, all the way to well-known and beloved Swing pieces.

Jacky Sevilya

A world-renowned virtuoso player and composer, extraordinary expressive abilities and a rich record of experience performing worldwide. His playing serves as inspiration to musicians in Israel and around the world.

Ilan Ziv

A young and captivating musician - discovered as a "prodigy" and quickly began playing as a soloist in various ensembles. A graduate of the Jerusalem Academy of Music, specializing in world music. Considered a leader of the young generation of musicians.
